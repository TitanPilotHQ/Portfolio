# Investor Data Room — 04. Investor FAQ

Anticipated questions, honestly answered, each pointing to the canonical
source rather than restating it (Global Constraint 4). This file's own
content is limited to the questions themselves and the routing — not new
facts.

**"How much have you raised?"**
Nothing to date. See `03-cap-table-and-funding.md`.

**"What's your cap table?"**
Sole founder ownership, no legal entity yet. See `03-cap-table-and-funding.md`.

**"Do you have customers?"**
No paying customers. Titan Pilot is engaging (not yet confirmed) design
partners during a certified shadow-mode validation phase. See Module 01's
Company stage section (`01-brand-bible/01-identity.md`) and
`02-traction-and-evidence.md`'s "what's explicitly NOT evidence of" section.

**"What's your revenue?"**
None — pre-revenue. See `05-pricing-system/01-pricing-philosophy.md`.

**"What's your pricing model?"**
Directionally: B2B SaaS, annual, per-desk/workspace, enterprise custom — an
internal working assumption, not a finalized public commitment. See
`05-pricing-system/02-pricing-model-structure.md`. Module 05's own decision
tree (`05-pricing-system/03-pricing-decision-tree.md`) gates this as
internal-only regardless of audience, including investors, "until Emad
explicitly clears it for public statement" — an investor conversation is not
automatically an exception. If Emad wants to disclose the model direction to
a specific investor conversation, that's a specific clearance decision to
make at the time, not a standing rule this file grants in advance.

**"Who's your competition?"**
See `04-positioning/02-competitive-positioning-map.md` for the real,
8-competitor comparison, and `04-positioning/01-category-positioning.md` for
why Titan Pilot doesn't fit any existing category cleanly.

**"What's your total addressable market?"**
Not yet quantified in any sourced material. Module 15 (Market Research
Framework) owns market-sizing methodology once built, and per Global
Constraint 1, no number should be offered here or invented for this
conversation until Module 15 has real, sourced data.

**"Who's on the team?"**
Founder-led, intentionally small. See Module 01's Company stage section
(`01-brand-bible/01-identity.md`) and, for any future hiring plan, Module 13
(Hiring OS).

**"What's your moat / why can't a competitor just copy this?"**
Not a question this file answers with a new claim — route to the actual
architecture facts (`lib/content.ts:421-438`, "AI cannot bypass execution,"
"Dependencies point one way," etc., already the subject of Module 02's
Technical/partner audience variant, `02-story-architecture/
02-audience-variants.md`) rather than a generic defensibility argument this
module would have to invent.

**"When will you raise / are you raising now?"**
Not currently. See `03-cap-table-and-funding.md`'s Phase B framing — the
honest, complete answer, not a deflection.

**"What happens if [key person] leaves — is this a key-person risk?"**
Yes, honestly — Titan Pilot is currently a single-founder company (Module 01
Company stage). This file does not soften that into a team-based framing
that isn't true yet.

## Maintenance

Add a question here only once it's been asked in a real conversation (or is
obviously certain to be, like the ones above) — this file doesn't attempt to
anticipate every conceivable investor question in advance.
